Ball Game

For the Undertale theorists reading this, the "soul traits" commonly theorized simply do not exist in Undersided; the intended purpose of the Ball Game is to memorialize the personality qualities of the six humans who were slain by yarov as part of Ruaeli's plan to escape the Underground. This song was made to emphasize that point, sharing a melody with the direct appearance of the SOULs.
